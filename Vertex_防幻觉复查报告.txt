### 2. 【核心机制深度解析】

*   **界面稳定性与副反应抑制 (XPS & HRTEM)**
    *   **机理:** LTZP 包覆层物理隔绝了电极与电解液，有效抑制了碳酸酯溶剂分解和富镍正极表面晶格氧的脱出，从而稳定了界面结构。
    *   **原文支撑 (XPS):** "In comparison with NCM-0, weaker peak intensities of the corresponding characteristic peaks could be detected in the C 1 s spectra of NCM-LTZP1, indicating that the formation of Li2CO3 and other carbonates was substantially inhibited in the presence of the LTZP buffer layer... Therefore, its interfacial structure was highly stable, and it substantially inhibited the escape of lattice oxygen (i.e., TMs dissolution)."
    *   **原文支撑 (HRTEM):** "Unlike the R3-m phase existing in the NCM-0 surface, an orthorhombic phase with an inter-planar of 2.46 Å, corresponding to the (002) plane of LTZP, and a thickness of ~20 nm was exhibited in the surface of NCM-LTZP1 (region III)... The HAADF-STEM images confirmed the formation of lattice-matched LTZP coating on NCM811 secondary particles, revealing the epitaxial growth behavior of LTZP along the NCM811 lattice."

*   **结构稳定性与相变抑制 (XRD & dQ/dV)**
    *   **机理:** Ti/Zr 掺杂形成的强 Ti-O/Zr-O 化学键增强了过渡金属层的稳定性，抑制了高充电态下从 H2 到 H3 的有害相变及其伴随的晶格坍缩，从而减缓了微裂纹的产生。
    *   **原文支撑 (dQ/dV):** "For NCM-LTZP1, the three phase transitions remained essentially unchanged, indicating that the H2/H3 phase transition is suppressed. This is due to the strong Ti-O/Zr-O bonding stabilizing the TM-O layer, while the LTZP coating moderates the solid electrolyte interface and enhances the lithium ion migration."

*   **电子/离子导电性增强 (DFT)**
    *   **机理:** LTZP 的引入（特别是 Zr 的 3d 轨道）增加了费米能级附近的电子态密度，同时 Ti/Zr 掺杂形成的强化学键降低了体系的总能量，增强了结构稳定性。功函数的降低则表明电子更容易从费米能级溢出，共同促进了电子电导率的提升。
    *   **原文支撑 (DFT - 结合能):** "The BE of the system after partial substitution of Ti and Zr atoms for the Ni sites in NCM ternary materials was calculated to be 8.25 eV by using Equation (1), indicating that the introduction of Ti and Zr atoms made the NCM ternary materials more stable."
    *   **原文支撑 (DFT - 态密度):** "...the introduction of 3d orbital Zr atoms into NCM811 after LTZP surface modification remarkably increased the density of electronic states near the Fermi energy level, thus increasing the electron concentration and enhancing the conductivity."
